Together we can build a Utopian Playground

Welcome to the website of the great Doctor Phineas Waldorf Steel: Creator, Entertainer, Visionary.

Dr. Steel was a man who used his incredible talents to create, entertain, and enlighten, spreading his message across the globe. A scholar and a philosopher, he covered such topics as transhumanism, freedom of thought, and subjective reality. His goal was to achieve nothing less than total world domination, converting the world as we know it into a Utopian Playland, where fun is the top priority. He created elegant music, striking images, and thought-provoking videos, and enlisted ordinary citizens just like yourself into his Army of Toy Soldiers to spread his message and further the cause.

Sadly, in 2011 Dr. Steel decided to retire from the entertainment world and refocus his efforts elsewhere, confirmed by the head of the Army of Toy Soldiers in early 2012. Despite the retirement of Dr. Steel himself may have retired, his army of toy soldiers continue to grow, spreading the message of 'Making Fun The Top Priority', even without the man who started it all.

The Army of Toy Soldiers, now known as Toy Soldiers Unite, continue on spreading the philosophy of Dr. Steel. Encouraging other independent artists and entertainers, the Army regularly participates in events such as San Diego Comic Con, MCM Expo, and the Pasedena Doo Dah Parade. The Army also regularly participates in charity events benefiting organizations such as Toys for Tots, Light the Night Walk, Adopt a Highway, and Child's Play.

With your help, we can continue to grow, spreading the philosophy of Dr. Steel, and help make our world a Utopian Playground. So what are you waiting for, soldier? Enlist today!
